About

At the Intersection of
Industry & Intelligence

I am a results-driven Beverage Industry Executive and Machine Learning Engineer specializing at the intersection of operational excellence and automated intelligence. My career spans from managing complex brewery and restaurant group operations to architecting AI-driven systems that transform raw market data into scalable commercial growth.

Leveraging deep domain expertise in beverage supply chains and premium portfolio launches, I now build end-to-end data pipelines and ML models designed to solve real-world operational friction. Whether it's optimizing distributor accountability through predictive analytics or deploying automated content engines for niche markets, my focus is on replacing manual bottlenecks with high-integrity digital workflows.

Technical Focus: Machine Learning Architecture, LLM Orchestration, Automated Market Intelligence, and Data-Informed Commercial Strategy.
